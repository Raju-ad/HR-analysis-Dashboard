# HR Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an **HR Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition trends.
The dashboard offers detailed insights across age group, education, job role, salary slab, and years at the company, enabling HR teams to make data-driven decisions for employee retention and workforce planning.

## Key Insights

- **Highest Attrition by Age:** 26–35 year group

- **Job Role Impact:** Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives are most affected

- **Education Influence:** Highest attrition among Life Sciences and Medical graduates

- **Salary Slab:** Most attrition from employees earning up to ₹5K

- **Experience:** Maximum attrition seen in employees with 0–1 years of service

- **Gender Distribution:** Male employees show higher attrition count
---

## Dashboard Components

1. **KPI Tiles** – Overview of attrition metrics

2. **Pie Charts** – Attrition by education

3. **Bar Charts** – Attrition by salary, age, job role

4. **Trend Graph** – Attrition by years at company

5. **Matrix View** – Job role vs years of service comparison

6. **Interactive Filters** – Category-wise exploration
